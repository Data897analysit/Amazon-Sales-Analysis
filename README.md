## 📌 Project Overview

This Amazon Sales Analysis project provides a **comprehensive overview of key performance indicators** such as profit, revenue, shipping days, and unit sales.

The analysis focuses on:
- 📍 Evaluating **profit distribution** across regions and countries
- 📡 Identifying **channel-specific revenue trends** (Online vs Offline)
- 📅 Understanding **yearly variations** in orders and costs
- 🧾 Examining **item-wise metrics** — revenue, profit margins, shipments, units sold

By uncovering actionable insights, this study enables Amazon to enhance regional performance, streamline operations, and drive sustainable growth.

---

## 🎯 Background & Goals

### Background
Amazon, as a global e-commerce leader, processes millions of transactions daily across various regions, channels, and categories. Understanding sales trends and performance metrics is crucial for sustaining profitability and operational efficiency.

### Goals
> Evaluate the impact of shipping days, order priorities, and sales across different products and regions — ultimately guiding decision-making for operational improvements.

### 🚚 Revenue & Shipment Days Analysis

## 🚀 Next Steps & Recommendations

### 🌍 Regional Strategy
- ✅ Capitalize on high demand in **Sub-Saharan Africa & Europe**
- ✅ Conduct **regional demand analysis** to tailor products
- ✅ Identify factors causing **low profitability in India & North America**

### 🛒 Channel Strategy
- ✅ Improve **online customer experience** — product quality, shipping, pricing
- ✅ Boost offline sales via **targeted discounts & retention strategies**

### 📦 Product Strategy
- ✅ Expand **Cosmetics** line into new market segments
- ✅ Investigate high costs in **Office Supplies & Household**
- ✅ Reconsider **Fruits** strategy — new pricing, range expansion, or discontinue
- ✅ Improve **marketing for Baby Food & Clothes**

### 🚚 Shipping Optimization
- ✅ Optimize **delivery routes** and leverage faster shipping methods
- ✅ Reduce shipping times where averages are rising
- ✅ Expand the **delivery network**
